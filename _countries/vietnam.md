    From China we head south, crossing the border into Vietnam, hugging the eastern coast along the South China Sea to the historic old-town trading port of Hoi An, a UNESCO Heritage Site. 
    
    Here, we step back in time to the 16th and 17th centuries, where merchant vessels from Japan, China, Portugal, and the Netherlands all crowd the docks along the Thu Bồn River, eager to trade fine silks, spices, and porcelain. 
    
    It is against this backdrop that we play the game Fai Fo - a card game of risk, deduction and surprise as you seek to outmaneuver your competition, amass a fortune, and secure three exclusive royal trading contracts from the King of Đại Việt.

    Playing the Highest Reputation Merchant will give you money but only the lowest merchant can trigger its special Skill. The Merchant Ship counting as reputation 0 may give you the chance to earn a great deal but be aware, if there are too many Merchant Ship sailing, there will be no money for anyone.
