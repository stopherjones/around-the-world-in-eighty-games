    From the historic, lantern-lit trading ports of Vietnam, our journey heads south across the Gulf of Thailand, to the island city-state of Singapore.

    Bursting out of dense jungle, Singapore is renowned as a global financial hub and multicultural melting pot that seamlessly blends futuristic skyscrapers with lush nature parks and vibrant historic neighborhoods.

    Marina Bay is Singapore’s futuristic waterfront district, built on reclaimed land to serve as the city’s financial and tourism hub. Famous for its iconic skyline, upscale dining, luxury shopping, and pedestrian-friendly promenades that offer panoramic views of the city.
    
    But our stop here is far from decadent relaxation - we'll be climbing Marina Bay Sands, the iconic resort complex comprising three 57-storey towers, connected by Sands Skypark, a 340-metre-long (1,120 ft) skyway connecting the towers with a capacity of 3,902 people and a 150 m (490 ft) infinity swimming pool.

    Our game here is Roll to the Top Journeys, a deceptively simple push your luck game with a roll n write mechanic. Each turn, the dice are rolled, and everyone simultaneously uses the numbers to fill in the grid of their landmark from the bottom up. The catch? Every single number you write must be equal to or greater than the number in the square directly beneath it. Do you start with low numbers at the base to give yourself plenty of room to grow? Or do you take a risk on a high roll to rocket up a vertical shaft, praying you don't box yourself in later?
